About

Few words about The Neighborhood Care.

What we believe

We are a group of young people working together to create a chain of networks, collaborations, and partnerships and encourage a culture of engagement, discussion and care for their own neighborhoods. We believe that if everybody start to care for their neighbour in their neighbourhood, it can make everybody’s lives better. We work across various issues inclusive of education, livelihood, and neighbourhood development and care.
What we believe: We believe that a lot of the societal issues could be solved when the existing pool of talents and resources available in the locality are drawn together and channel it in the right direction. The young people are smart and intelligent. All they need is a tap on their shoulder, a call to a cause, or an environment that nurtures. And The Neighborhood Care is a medium to channel those wishes and dreams by helping connect with the right people to create the change they want to see.

An Essay Competition

Education

ENGAGE WRITE AND WIN: ESSAY COMPETITION FOR SCHOOL STUDENTS Under covid-19, there are increased fear, confusion and also a threat to our collective society shaking our ethics and hospitality. This is not just a health emergency but also threatens many of our community ethics and making our society vulnerable to moral constraints and ruptures. In order to encourage engagement and discussions around such societal issues and promote community discernment in charting ways for us as a society, we are launching an essay competition for the students in Tamenglong Headquarter.
The theme for the competition is titled “Welcoming the returnees in Tamenglong: Are we hospitable?” The last date for the submission of the essay is Sunday 23rd August 2020. It is a single competition but students from class 6-8 can write around 500 words while class 9 and 10 can write around 600 words. There will be cash prizes of 2000, 1500, and 1000 rupees for the first three winners respectively and three consolation prizes of 500 rupees each for the next three winners.

  • Date: 23rd August 2020
  • Category: Education
